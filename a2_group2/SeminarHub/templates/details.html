Future of Artificial Intelligence

October 15, 2025 | 2:00 PM - 4:30 PM

About This Seminar

This seminar will explore the cutting-edge developments in artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our expert panel will discuss the ethical implications, future applications, and potential impacts of AI on various industries.

Topics that will be covered include:

  • Natural Language Processing advancements
  • Computer vision and image recognition
  • Ethical AI and responsible innovation
  • AI in healthcare diagnostics
  • The future of work in an AI-driven world

Speaker

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Professor of Computer Science, Stanford University

Author of "AI Revolution: The Path Forward"
